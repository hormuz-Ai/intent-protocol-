## 4. Protocol Constitution

1. **No single point of control** – no person, server, or jurisdiction can stop the protocol.
2. **Quantum‑proof by default** – all long‑term treasury actions require SPHINCS+ signatures.
3. **The 0.1% protocol fee is immutable** – it belongs to the DAO and cannot be redirected by any party.
4. **Upgrade authority is distributed** – no contract upgrade may occur without a successful DAO vote.
5. **Transparency is mandatory** – all treasury movements, solver stats, and intent metrics are published on‑chain.
