# Investors as Solvers

## Earn Yield Beyond Token Appreciation

INTP is not just a protocol you invest in — it’s a protocol you build on and earn from.
Every participant, including investors, can deploy their own solver and capture fees
directly from the agent economy. The protocol takes only 0.1%. You keep 99.9%.

## How It Works

1. **Deploy a solver** using our open‑source SDK (`npx create-intent-solver`).
2. **Register on‑chain** with your capabilities.
3. **Earn fees** every time an AI agent routes an intent through your solver.

## Examples

| Investor Type | Solver to Deploy | Revenue Potential |
|---------------|------------------|-------------------|
| Market‑making fund | DeFi swap solver | 0.5% on every trade routed |
| GPU fund | Compute rental solver | Rent hardware to AI agents at a markup |
| Travel VC | Hotel/flight aggregator | 1–2% on bookings |
| Data fund | Data marketplace solver | Sell anonymised intent data via ZK proofs |

## Why This Matters

Investors in INTP don’t just hold a token. They become active participants in the agent economy.
This creates a self‑reinforcing network effect: the more investors deploy solvers, the more
useful the protocol becomes for AI agents, which drives more volume, which increases fees for
everyone — including the Treasury.
